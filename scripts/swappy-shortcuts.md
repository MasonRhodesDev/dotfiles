# рҹҡҖ Swappy Keyboard Shortcuts

## рҹ“Ӣ **Clipboard & Saving (What you wanted!)**
- **`Ctrl+C`** - Copy to clipboard AND quit вӯҗ
- **`Ctrl+S`** - Save to file
- **`Escape`** or `q` or `Ctrl+W` - Quit (manual)

## рҹҺЁ **Drawing Tools**
- `b` - Brush
- `t` or `e` - Text
- `r` or `s` - Rectangle  
- `c` or `o` - Ellipse
- `a` - Arrow
- `d` - Blur

## рҹҺЁ **Colors & Styling**
- `R` - Red color
- `G` - Green color  
- `B` - Blue color
- `C` - Custom color
- `+` - Increase stroke size
- `-` - Decrease stroke size
- `=` - Reset stroke size
- `f` - Toggle shape filling
- `T` - Toggle transparency

## вҡҷпёҸ **Controls**
- `Ctrl+Z` - Undo
- `Ctrl+Shift+Z` or `Ctrl+Y` - Redo
- `x` or `k` - Clear all drawings
- `Ctrl+B` - Toggle paint panel
- `Ctrl` (hold) - Center shapes from start point

## рҹ”Ҙ **Workflow Tips:**
1. Take screenshot with `snip-swappy`
2. Draw your annotations
3. **`Ctrl+C`** to copy AND quit вӯҗ
4. Done! рҹҺҜ
